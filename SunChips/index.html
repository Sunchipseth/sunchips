The Company

Senselet Food Processing PLC is a company that aims to become market leader in high quality potato products in Ethiopia with a positive impact on food security. Our developing organization of around 170 people works in the full potato chain from farmer until consumer with the purpose to win through high quality, professionalism and a multicultural social environment. In coming years we are evolving our company by increasing our farmer reach, increasing our production and product varieties and delivering in more cities in Ethiopia. We aim to be market leader in potato products. We want to achieve this by investing in the potato chain starting at farmers via financial incentives, resources and knowledge (from Holland, a specialist in potatoes) ...

After that we want to have the highest quality products by selecting top quality potatoes, investing in the highest quality machinery and having the highest standards in production processes. As we are a social company wanting to have a positive impact on food security, our purpose is also to have an organization as such. We believe in winning through a constantly developing organization, working together in a professional and social way with diverse and happy people.

Our Vistion

SUN Chips’ vision is to help improve the yields and income of Ethiopian potato farmers as the #1 snacks manufacturer improving Standard, Safety and Health of snacks for all Ethiopians via 100% Quality products.

Our Mission

SUN Chips’ mission is to facilitate inputs, provide training and give continuous assistance to Ethiopian potato farmers, and produce 100% Quality SUN Chips, in the best flavors bringing Your SUNny Moment for all Ethiopians, every day.

Where to Buy

You don’t have to go too far to get your SUNNY moment. SUN Chips’ are available at any supermarket and any of your local stores all across Ethiopia.
